Privacy Policy – QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner
Effective Date: 29 January 2026
Last Updated: 9 March 2026
QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner, developed by Rashid Khan Tech, handles information when you use the application.
Information We Collect
Personal Information
QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner does NOT collect, store, or require any personal information.
We do not collect:
Name
Email address
Phone number
Location data
Any personal identification information
Permissions Required
Camera Permission
Camera access is required only to scan QR codes and barcodes.
Used solely for scanning purposes
Camera data is processed in real time
No camera data is recorded, stored, or saved
Camera data is not shared, uploaded, or transmitted to any server
Storage & Media Permission
Storage permission is used only to:
Save generated QR codes to your device
Save scanned barcode or QR images
Allow sharing of QR codes and barcodes as images
QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner does NOT access, read, or modify any other files on your device.
Scan History & Favorites
All scanned data, generated QR codes, scan history, and favorites are stored locally on your device only.
No data is uploaded
No cloud storage or backup
No syncing across devices
No server-side storage
All data remains entirely on your device.
How We Use Information
QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner uses local data only to:
Scan QR codes and barcodes
Generate QR codes
Save QR and barcode images locally
Display scan history and favorites on your device
Data Sharing & Third Parties
QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner follows a strict no-sharing policy:
We do NOT sell your data
We do NOT share data with advertisers
We do NOT use analytics or tracking SDKs
We do NOT integrate third-party services that collect personal or behavioral data
The app does not collect, process, or share any user data as defined under Google Play’s User Data policies.
Internet Usage
QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner does NOT send any user data over the internet.
Internet access is used only when you choose to open content from a scanned QR code or barcode, such as:
Websites
Email links
Phone or messaging actions
These actions are initiated entirely by the user.
Privacy for Users
QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner is designed for a general audience.
The app:
Does not collect personal data
Does not store personal data
Does not process personal data
Because no personal data is handled, the app complies with Google Play privacy and data safety requirements.
Data Security
QR Scannora – Barcode Scanner ensures strong privacy protection by design:
100% on-device local storage
No cloud synchronization
No external servers
No background data transmission
Your data stays on your device at all times.

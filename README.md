# Scannora_policy
Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 29 January 2026
Scanora respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how Scanora handles information when you use the application.
Information We Collect
1. Personal Information
Scanora does NOT collect, store, or require any personal information.
We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any form of personal identification.
2. Camera Permission
Camera access is required only to scan QR codes and barcodes.
Camera access is used solely for scanning purposes
Camera data is processed in real time
No camera data is recorded, stored, or saved
Camera data is not shared, uploaded, or transmitted to any server
3. Storage & Media Permission
Storage permission is used to:
Save generated QR codes to your device
Save scanned barcode or QR images
Allow sharing of QR codes and barcodes as images
Scanora does NOT access, read, or modify any other files on your device.
4. Scan History & Favorites
All scanned data, generated QR codes, scan history, and favorites are stored locally on your device only.
No data is uploaded
No cloud storage or backup is used
No syncing across devices
No server-side storage
All data remains entirely on your device.
How We Use Information
Scanora uses local data only to:
Scan QR codes and barcodes
Generate QR codes
Save QR and barcode images locally
Display scan history and favorites on your device
Data Sharing & Third Parties
Scanora follows a strict no-sharing policy:
We do NOT sell your data
We do NOT share data with advertisers
We do NOT use analytics or tracking SDKs
We do NOT integrate third-party services that collect personal or behavioral data
Internet Usage
Scanora does not send any user data over the internet.
Internet access is used only when you choose to open content from a scanned QR code or barcode, such as:
Websites
Email links
Phone or messaging actions
These actions are initiated entirely by the user.
Privacy for All Users
Scanora is designed for users of all ages.
No personal data is collected from any user
No age-based data processing occurs
No consent for personal data collection is required
Because no personal information is collected, stored, or shared, Scanora complies with general privacy requirements for all age groups.
Data Security
Scanora ensures strong data privacy by design:
100% on-device local storage
No cloud synchronization
No external servers
No background data transmission
Your data stays on your device at all times.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect app updates or legal requirements.
Any changes will be made available within the app or on the app store listing.
